Bitcoin Snapshots     


LSDE 2021 - Group 26


Our project examines a total of 702,460 Bitcoin blocks from 2009 to 2021.
Transaction graphs are constructed at multiple time points and several indicative
graph metrics such as size, degree distribution, PageRank and account balance are
computed to show how the properties of Bitcoin blockchain evolve over time.

Number of Edges and Vertices

Several basic parameters of the transaction graph, such as the number of edges, the number of vertices, and the average degree, are shown here.



Degree Distribution (2009 - 2016)

Here we show the top 100 degree distribution of transaction graph from 2009 to 2016, presented on a logarithmic coordinate.



PageRank (2009 - 2012)

Here we show the top 100 Pagerank of transactions from 2009 to 2012, and you can hover your mouse over the bar to see the corresponding Bitcoin address.



Balance (Top 100 Addresses)

Here we show the top 100 bitcoin account balances from 2010 to the end of 2016, in billionths of a bitcoin. You can hover your mouse over the bar chart to see the corresponding Bitcoin address. You can also switch between snapshots to see the results.
